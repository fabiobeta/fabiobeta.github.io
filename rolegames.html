Adventurs & Campaigns

Across the centuries, I have created many adventures that I am always happy to play again. If any of these excite you... get in touch and we can plan a game

The Claw of Clearwater

My oldest Chtulhu adventure. Great to bring new detectives into the world of the mythos. It starts with a trip to a remote New England village, and then steamrolls into mystery as the killing spree of a bear becomes the least possible explanation to the strangeness overload.

The Dwarven Ring

I wrote this on the excitement of the first season of The Rings of Power. I humbly said "I could do better".

It plays with the rules of One Ring, second edition. Set somewhere in the second age, it attacks the problem of the rings. It places a gray twist on the heroes. They start with their agenda: having stolen the knowledge to forge s magic ring... they find themselves as keepersvof a terrible secret, and then as defenders of the middle earth, with the eye looking for them.

Yes, I did better
